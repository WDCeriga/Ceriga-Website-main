Frequently asked questions

Got questions? We’ve got answers. Explore our FAQs to
learn more about how Ceriga works, what we offer, and how
we support your brand. Find quick solutions and helpful
insights all in one place.

Ceriga is an all-in-one platform for clothing brands and designers. We provide the digital tools and resources you need to streamline the entire production process, from initial design to final delivery. Our service is perfect for brands of all sizes looking for greater control, quality, and efficiency.

We offer extensive customization for a wide range of apparel. Our platform allows you to control every detail, including editable measurement tables, diverse colour palettes, specific fabric selections, and tailored packaging and labelling.

We specialize in on-demand production, which means you can order the exact quantity you need, whenever you need it. This gives you the flexibility to test new products and manage inventory without large financial commitments.

A digital tech pack is a comprehensive blueprint of your garment. Our tools allow you to create a detailed digital file that documents every technical specification—from measurements and materials to finishing instructions. This ensures clear communication with manufacturers and guarantees consistent, accurate production every time.

We offer reliable, worldwide shipping. Once your custom apparel is complete, we handle the logistics to ensure your products are delivered securely and efficiently, wherever you and your customers are located.

Our pricing is based on the specific garment, customization options, and quantity you order. Our platform provides transparent, real-time quotes as you design, so you always know the cost upfront with no hidden fees.

We work with a curated network of vetted manufacturing partners around the world. We select our partners based on their ethical practices, quality standards, and ability to meet the unique needs of your collection. Our platform provides transparency, giving you peace of mind about where and how your garments are made.

We offer a curated selection of high-quality blank garments, including premium hoodies, t-shirts, joggers, and more. Our range provides the perfect canvas for your unique designs and is built to be a consistent base for any collection.
